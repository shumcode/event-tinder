Welcome to Date-Off!

Tired of arguing over what to do for date night? Let Date-Off choose it for you!

Step 1: Player 1 chooses the type of activities or events that he/she want to do.


Step 2: Player 2 chooses the type of activities or events that he/she want to do.


Step 3: After both players have chosen their activities, one random date will be chosen for the couple.
